    Home page

  1. Hold your phone or tablet horizontally
  2. Click 'Start' button to start training
  3. Use 'Contact me' button to contact the author

    Training page

  1. Click the bottom bar to change to next card
    if you are using computer, press:
    • z or arrow right - to change to next card
    • x or arrow up - to change to the next three cards

  2. After memorizing all the cards, you have 5 minutes to recall and submit the cards in the correct order on the page, by click:
    • The suits button - to change the suit,
    • The cards - to select the card,
    • click the boxes - to select the place you want to put the next card,
    • or, double click(or drag the card on mobile device) - to take the card down
    if you finished , click the Finish button (or if the time is up), it will goes to the page that shows you the answer

  3. In the show answer page, you can see your score and time.
    If you want to see the correct answer, click Original button.

  4. You can Hide the time of the timer by click the menu on the left up corner and then click the Hide time button.
